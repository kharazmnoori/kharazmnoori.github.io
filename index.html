Who I am.

Hello, my name is Kharazm Noori, and I’m a passionate learner. It all started when I was a kid and got curious about the technology behind game development. Soon I was looking up tutorials on how to manipulate the values in my favorite video game, GTA®, using Cheat Engine.

Over the years, I've deepened my passion for computer science by studying IT. During my undergraduate program in Iran, I especially focused on web development, since i wanted to create interactive, visually appealing websites. Lately, I’ve discovered a new passion in the world of Machine-Learning and all the possibilities it can unlock.

I’m now looking forward to finally beginn my career and learn among professionals who also dedicated their lives to similar passions and interests.
